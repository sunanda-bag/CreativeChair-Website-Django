An accent chair, can be an extra place to sit but it can be much more. It is a way to reveal a color of your personality, arouse curiosity and why not envy. Clever eclectic mixes are popular in a warm decor.

Each chair is unique, it is created in our workshop. The choice of fabrics, the solidity of the chairs, its originality, its playful and artistic aspect are essential elements for us.

A second life is given to beautiful old chairs. They are covered with whimsical and original fabrics. The backs of some chairs are hand painted by our artist Joanne.

Who are we? We are two sisters, Johanne and Martine, passionate about original and heterogeneous decor arrangements. During the pandemic, we were looking for a project to combine our experience, our originality and our talents. The idea of ​​creating unique accent chairs that become distinctive decorative objects that you love at first glance has become our creative goal. We fall in love with every chair.

For us, this is the guarantee that you will like them.
